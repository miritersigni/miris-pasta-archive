About

Hello! :)


I’m Miriana, but my friends call me Miri!


Born and raised in Italy, I’ve always had a deep love and appreciation for the wonderful and rich world of pasta that makes Italian cuisine so special. Growing up, I spent countless hours in the kitchen with my nonna, learning the art of making pasta from scratch. From working the dough to shaping each piece with care, pasta became more than just food: it turned into a way to connect with my roots and create precious memories with the people I love. I realized I wanted to share this love with others. This website is my way of doing that, offering a fun and informative resource for all pasta lovers curious to learn more about the most famous and unique shapes. Whether it’s long, short, filled, or in soup, pasta is always a true expression of Italian culture, with each shape telling its own unique story.


I hope this space brings a piece of Italy to your table!


Miriana
