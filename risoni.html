Risoni is a type of small pasta shaped like a large grain of rice. In fact, the name “risoni” translates to “big rice”, reflecting the shape’s rice-like appearance. This small, oval-shaped pasta is traditionally made from semolina flour, derived from durum wheat. This small pasta shape is favored due to its ability to maintain a firm, al dente texture after cooking, maintaining a pleasing texture in all dishes. Risoni’s shape and size make them ideal for various soup recipes.
