Tortellini is a traditional Italian stuffed pasta characterized by its distinctive ring shape, reminiscent of a small folded ring or a navel. These small rings are created by folding square or circular pasta sheets around a filling and twisting it into their characteristic form. Traditional tortellini fillings include a mixture of meats such as pork loin, prosciutto, and mortadella combined with Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. They are commonly served in a rich beef broth, especially popular during festive occasions like Christmas. Tortellini is also enjoyed with a variety of sauces, including tomato-based, cream, or pesto.
